What is Medicare Advantage?

Medicare Advantage plans, commonly known as Medicare Part C, give the same coverage as Medicare Parts A and B, plus many more benefits that are provided at no additional cost. Unless you are eligible for a Special Enrollment Period, your enrollment may be restricted to specific periods of the year.

Most plans include prescription drug coverage.

Many plans may include dental, vision or hearing coverage.

$0 or low premium and $0 deductible plans may be available in your area.
